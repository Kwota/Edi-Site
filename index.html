ABOUT

Acrobatic Choreographer / Event Designer / Assistant Director

Edesia Moreno Barata started her performance career as an elite athlete with the Rhythmic Gymnastics Spanish National Team and is now a leading Acrobatic Choreographer and Assistant Director.

Edesia started first as an artist and toured for 10 years participating in the productions of Saltimbanco and Corteo with the renowned CIRQUE DU SOLEIL. With her contagious passion, flair for movement and her ability to deliver results, Edesia evolved into the positions of Artistic and Acrobatic coach at the International Head Quarters of Cirque du Soleil in Montreal.

Most recently, Edesia has served as an Acrobatic Choreographer and Assistant Stage Director for many new creations and special events with Cirque du Soleil and other leading circus companies in Canada, such a 7 DOIGTS DE LA MAIN and 45DEGREES.

Edesia has spent 18 years in variety of key roles for renown companies and participated in 30 creations for the world stage as of now, such as LUZIA newest creation of Cirque Du Soleil.

Edesia brings to the table a unique combination of organization, team collaboration, flexibility, respect, leadership, creativity and passion.

Edesia is always ready to be challenged, there are no limits to Edesia's will to be challenged.

To be creative is freedom of expression beyond imagination and a creative adult is a child that has awakened
